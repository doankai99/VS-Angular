YOUR INITIAL CONSULTATION GETTING TO KNOW YOU

At the beginning of your consultation, our tailoring consultant will sit with you and discuss
what you’re hoping to get from a suit and if you have any images or ideas you’ve brought with you.
Your answers to these questions will give us the information we need to show you our recommended cloth books and get started on the style.

Learn More

We have thousand cloths to choose from, so there should be something for everyone’s taste and requirements. At your consultation, you can browse through our cloth books at your leisure.
Our tailoring consultant will be on hand to guide you through our huge array of fabrics.

Once you have chosen a cloth that you like, we will show you our lining selection. Whether you like classic neutrals, bold colour or quirky prints, we have the perfect lining for you.
